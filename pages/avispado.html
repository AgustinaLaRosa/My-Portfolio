RESEARCH · UX DESIGN · UI DESIGN

Project Overview

This was my first UX/UI project which I started from scratch, it is all my own creation. It is about an App where you can rent different kinds of products/elements.
The problem/need was "people want to save money by avoiding unnecessary purchases. Also, people want to make money with things they have at home and don't use all the time". The solution I found was to create an app where I can connect people that want to save money by renting from the people that want to generate extra money by lending their belongings. I started by doing research about similar services on the market, analyzing those findings, validating them with users, and finishing with the design of the app.
