Tinder App.
UX/UI Redesign

RESEARCH · UX DESIGN · UI DESIGN

Project Overview

Although Tinder one of the most popular dating application in the world, it currently does not have some features which creates frustration for users. The objective of this research is to understand and find pain points through different UX tools. Then, with this data, provide solutions to the needs of users and thus optimize the application to improve the experience
